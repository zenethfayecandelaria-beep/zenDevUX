ABOUT THE WEBSITE

Zen began working on this website in December 2025, starting with just a concept for her special someone. Unfortunately, construction was paused due to her busy schedule. Later, she resumed the project with a completely new concept. This website was meant to serve as a Valentine's gift, but things didn't go exactly as planned. Despite these setbacks, she continued building the website from scratch.


It's funny how she kept changing the concept until she finally settled on the current idea for her proposed website. She created this concept on January 26. Coding began on January 28, and the construction continued until February 12. She remained comitted to keeping the concept simple, believing it would be too complicated to create such a masterpiece in just a week.

Even with everything happened, she thought about giving it to her special someone. Now, she is determined that this website will be her gift, a simple yet memorable one. She justs want her special someone to know that she still cares.
